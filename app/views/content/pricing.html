Monthly Price

$40 per month per user account

an additional $10 per month if you'd like your personal assistant / secretary to have a login ID tied to your account*

an additional $10 per month for Premium Support**

*
Note: the Assistant Account only pays one monthly fee regardless of how many User Accounts the Assitant Account is linked to (read more)
**
Before you sign up for Premium Support please take a look at our Support section we think our demo's, documentation, and forums are sufficient that you don't need to spend the extra money
Philosophy

What does philosophy have to do with pricing? Nothing really except that we believe that our customers are probably a lot like us. For example,

we don't want to provide our contact information just to see what a product looks like
we don't want an annual commitment
just tell us the price we don't want a salesperson to contact us
we can think for ourselves and don't need to be sold
we want to cancel at any time. Easily and for any reason.
So we hope that our pricing plan reflects our philosophy and that our service is easy to understand. However, if you'd like to speak with a customer service representative, or if you're simply curious what the weather is like in Cheyenne, feel free to contact us and we'll get you squared away.
