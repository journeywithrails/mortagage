no effort, easy to use, advanced analytics
After you import your loan origination files, Mortgage Science begins the process of analyzing each of your Borrower records for new loan opportunities. Each loan is calculated to determine the current balance, interest rate, next interest-rate change date, and current payment. The loans are then measured to determine if current rates or Borrower circumstances proivide an opportunity to refinance the loan or utilize excess home equity for debt consolidation, retirement or college planning, or purchasing a second home.

Loans are grouped by Borrower household and by property. A Borrower's complete loan history, list of properties, contact data, chronological history of marketing contacts, personalized news, and broker comments are all available at a glance.
