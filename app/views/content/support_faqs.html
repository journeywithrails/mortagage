What is Mortgage Science?
Mortgage Science is an innovative mortgage marketing and loan presentation product. We have developed a proprietary algorithm, called the Mortgage Assessment Engine, that searches through and analyzes your loan portfolio on a daily basis to identify new loan opportunities for your clients. Our system enables you to take advantage of these opportunities by automatically identifying the best clients to contact, performing all of the analysis required to determine a loan opportunity, and preparing presentation-ready reports that can be quickly sent to your clients for their review.


What Mortgage Science Cost?
Mortgage Science is a monthly subscription based service that costs $50 per month. There are no activation or termination fees, no minimum service period requirements, nor any other hidden fees. You are free to cancel at any time but by doing so, you may miss new loan opportunities that are identified for you each morning.

What Loan Origination Software Does Mortgage Science Support?
Mortgage Science can be used with either Calyx Point or Ellie Mae's Encompass program. With a few simple mouse clicks, you can upload your client files from your LOS into to our system. Once your data is transferred, we take care of the rest - there's no mapping of fields required.


Should I Stop Using My Loan Origination Software?
Mortgage Science is designed to be used in conjunction with your Loan Origination Software - there is no need to stop using your existing systems. While our service utilizes your existing Loan Origination files, we provide additional services and functions that your LOS system does not provide.


What Browswers and Operating Systems Does Mortgage Science Support?
At present, Mortgage Science can be used with Windows Operating Systems that utilize Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox.


Is Your Service Difficult To Use?
We've tried to design the Mortgage Science service to automate all of the tasks that make other mortgage marketing tools difficult to use. Furthermore, we've tried to make the user interface intuitive so that it is very easy to understand. However, if you ever have a question, please contact us and we'll be happy to answer any questions you may have.


Can I Customize The Reports?
Yes - you can upload both your logo as well as your picture in order to personalize the reports. In the near future, we will have additional personalization options for you to choose from.


How Do I Upload My Loan Origination Files?
When you're ready to uplod your LOS records, simply follow the instructions that we provide to you on-screen, and we'll take care of the rest.
